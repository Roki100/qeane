Qeane, a simple and powerful music bot!

You probably already have a music bot in your server like Groovy, so you may be asking "What is the point of adding **yet another one**?"" Well, the answer is surprisingly simple:

If some people are listening to something, but someone wants to listen to something else and don't want to disturb people, they can go to another voice channel and play this song with Qeane!

For now, Qeane supports the following sources for music:

  • YouTube links (songs & playlists)
  • SoundCloud links (i dont know about soundcloud but probably both too)
  • YouTube search

Spotify support will be added soon, the devs just need to find a way to do support playlists without spamming the API.

Qeane, a very good multi-purpose bot easy to use!

What is Qeane? It's a bot that has multiples features like moderation, fun commands and more! To get a list of commands, just type qeane help

This bot also offers tags, and here is how they work:
First, You create a tag with the tag-create command, like this: qeane tag-create sleep Please sleep more!

Second, you use the tag with qeane sleep and the bot will respond with "Please sleep more"! It's that easy! The tag-delete and tag-list commands are self-explanatory.


Tags are just one of the many features Qeane has, so dont don't hesitate to try it out!
